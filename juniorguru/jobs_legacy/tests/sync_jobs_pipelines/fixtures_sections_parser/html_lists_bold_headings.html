Main Project

UNMS is next-generation network management system for ISPs (Internet Service Providers). Current networks usually are both overly complicated and difficult to set up. You are required to possess extensive knowledge of network design and configuration of routers and switches. UNMS is here to simplify things, to empower almost anybody to start his own ISP business and to get Internet to 5 billion people still lacking it. As we speak UNMS is already used by more than 16.000 ISPs and it manages millions of devices world-wide.

More information and demo can be found on https://unms.com/.

Technologies used: React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, RabbitMQ, Docker
