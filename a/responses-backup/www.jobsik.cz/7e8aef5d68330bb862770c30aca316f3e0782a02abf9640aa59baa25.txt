                    <h2 class="job-item-detail__title">Požadavky</h2>
                    <div class="text">
                        We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer who is really interested in this area.<br />
<br />
We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer who is really interested in this area. We are introducing you the opportunity with a wide scope and what is the best - really early stage of the project - so your direct impact will be really significant. The choice of areas and technologies to fit your skills and interests - this is the opportunity which comes just once in a life :)<br />
Our client is a global company in the area of renewable energy. The company is building the next generation systems - energy systems powered by intelligent transactive markets, ubiquitous sensors, intensive analytics services.<br />
<br />
<br />
What is the opportunity about?<br />
   •  You could contribute to our project from a wide variety of areas:<br />
   •  Time series analysis and predictive models - generation forecasting, load forecasting, energy market price forecasting, analysis of sensor data across the whole energy value chain<br />
   •  Analytics services for virtual power plant/demand-side management platform<br />
   •  Anomaly detection and predictive maintenance for various assets in the energy grid<br />
   •  Development of automated energy trading agent intelligence - forecasts, trading strategies, backtests)<br />
   •  Development of energy-efficiency services based on data analytics<br />
   •  Cooperation with SW Engineers, energy experts, economists, product managers<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
You would be responsible for:<br />
   •   Data cleaning, exploratory analysis, visualization<br />
   •   Feature engineering, model building, training, evaluation<br />
   •   Machine learning applications - your product<br />
   •   You would be involved in the data-backend - stream processing and storage<br />
   •   Proposing and testing new hypotheses for potential services<br />
   •   Keeping up-to-date with development in machine learning and energy sector<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
What you should know:<br />
Data Science SW stack:<br />
   •   Python - numpy/scipy/matplotlib/sklearn/pandas/…<br />
   •   Deep learning - Tensorflow, Keras<br />
   •   Overview of machine learning and statistics<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
SW engineering skills<br />
   •   Linux, shell scripting, version control<br />
   •   Development of analytics - based on REST API services<br />
   •   Experience with cloud-providers - AWS, MS Azure, Google<br />
   •   SQL and NoSQL databases usage<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
Nice to have (not necessary) would be if you have an experience with…<br />
   •   Docker, Kubernetes<br />
   •   Time series databases<br />
   •   Practical experience with reinforcement learning (even advanced process control or trading - any area - commodities, crypto, equities)<br />
   •   Hadoop streaming-stack (Kafka, Spark/Flink)<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
What you can look for?<br />
   •  small team<br />
   •  international environment<br />
   •  new project and a wide scope of the opportunity for you.<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
So, isn´t it exciting? Willing to take part in this project? Let´s know about yourselves. Please send us your CV. Looking forward.<br />
