The purpose of the PL Manager application is to aggregate Front Office data and produce Profit&amp;Loss reports.<br><br>We aim to replace our current P&amp;L system (based on Sybase IQ) with a new application. This will be built using Oracle on database tier, Java on application tier and Angular on presentation tier.<br><br>The overall team size is 17, thereof 9 developers, 4 application support, and 4 business analysts. We provide a wide range of various IT solutions, .Net and Java development, services in the area of application operation and infrastructure.<br><br>We are looking for thinkers with unconventional ideas and in return, we offer the freedom to create own job with own vision, wide range of benefits including home office.
