    <h3><strong>Job Overview</strong></h3>
<p>Wargaming is growing their Prague based development studio and we’re looking for a Front-end Developer into the new team to work on projects for web/in-game browser and World of Tanks Portal.</p>
<p>Portfolio of the team includes such projects as:</p>
<p><strong>Landing Management System (LMS)</strong> - platform to develop and publish new landings to attract new audience and interact with existing one;</p>
<p><strong>World of Tanks Portal</strong> - main portal of the game (https://worldoftanks.eu);</p>
<p><strong>WebBridge</strong> - system to deliver personalized content into the client of World of Tanks;</p>
<p>All the project are adaptive and contain sophisticated animations. For the new projects team stick to component approach with usage of Vue.js and ES6.</p>
<p>As Front-end Developer you will focus on web and in-game projects for browser. Besides support of existing projects we also have a roadmap with new projects which should be created from scratch.</p>
<h3><strong>Reports To</strong></h3>
<p>Front-end Developer will report to the Engineering Manager.</p>
<h3><strong>What will you do?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Optimize and support existing projects like LMS, Portal and WebBridge;</li>
<li>Implement new projects from scratch;</li>
<li>Use latest HTML5 features to deploy modern responsive layouts with complex animations;</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What are we looking for?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in JS (ES5/ES6);</li>
<li>Experience with at least one component-based frameworks. Such as Vue.js (React, Angular, etc);</li>
<li>Experience in building front-end architecture;</li>
<li>Experience of supporting complex web projects;</li>
<li>Experience with adaptive and cross-browser layouts (IE11+);</li>
<li>Team player who enjoys cooperation with others;</li>
<li>Good English verbal and written communication skills;</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What additional skills will help you stand out?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Experience with preprocessors SASS or LESS;</li>
<li>Experience with Webpack or Gulp;</li>
<li>Knowledge how to develop Unit Tests;</li>
<li>Experience with SSR;</li>
<li>Experience with version control systems (Git, SVN);</li>
<li>Experience with template engine (Jinja2);</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>About Wargaming:</strong></h3>
<p>Wargaming is an award-winning online game developer and publisher headquartered in Nicosia, Cyprus. Operating since 1998, Wargaming has grown to become one of the leaders in the gaming industry with 5,500 employees and 18 offices spread all over the world. Over 200 million players enjoy Wargaming's titles across all major gaming platforms. Our flagship products include the free-to-play hits World of Tanks and World of Warships, as well as World of Tanks Blitz — the critically acclaimed mobile tank shooter with cross-platform support.</p>
<p>A career in our company is always an interesting challenge, as well as the experience that you gain working together with world-class experts.</p>
<p>Are you up to the challenge?!</p>
