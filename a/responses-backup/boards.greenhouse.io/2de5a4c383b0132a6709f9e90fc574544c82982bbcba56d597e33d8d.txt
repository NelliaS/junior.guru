  <h1 class='app-title'>Full-stack engineer</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Custom Ink

  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <p><strong>We are searching for a seasoned engineer that strives to deliver customer-centric apps. This position is suitable for a backend-focused engineer with a bit of a frontend knowledge. Someone who is curious beyond just the backend.</strong></p>
<p>At Custom Ink, we are building an e-commerce platform on which you are able to design and order custom printed t-shirts. We also develop the operations and supply chain tooling that powers our business and our large printing facilities.</p>
<p>Our team helps serve millions of customers. We build apps that make customers' lives easier by managing their own accounts and orders. We are also developing authentication solutions and following the latest OAuth security practices.</p>
<p><strong>What will you do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You'll join one of the Prague engineering teams.</li>
<li>Take a key part in<strong>&nbsp;designing the application architecture, making technology decisions, and developing new features</strong>.</li>
<li>Your responsibilities will range from product discovery, through implementation, to deployment and monitoring.&nbsp;</li>
<li>We want you to be involved in every part and argue or&nbsp;<strong>challenge us</strong>&nbsp;on a whiteboard.</li>
<li>You'll talk with your East Coast colleagues on a regular basis. Once the world gets back into place, we’ll want you to meet them in person.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What tools we use</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>We use primarily&nbsp;<strong>TypeScript</strong>&nbsp;(Nest.js) for developing new features. We also have applications written in<strong>&nbsp;Ruby on Rails</strong>&nbsp;that we keep building on.</li>
<li>We use Rails templates or&nbsp;<strong>React</strong>&nbsp;for the frontend part of the job.</li>
<li>We don't mind if you didn't use Ruby on Rails so far, we are constantly learning as well and will be&nbsp;<strong>happy to teach you our stack.</strong></li>
<li>We write meaningful tests and automate what we can.</li>
<li>We deploy mainly to the&nbsp;<strong>Kubernetes</strong>&nbsp;cluster and utilize Circle CI/CD pipelines.</li>
<li>We love&nbsp;<strong>AWS</strong>. We use RDS, EKS, ECR, CloudWatch, among others.</li>
<li>We also use&nbsp;<strong>NewRelic</strong>&nbsp;to monitor Apdex and performance monitoring.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What we offer</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary and equity packages.</li>
<li>Flexible working hours and home office.</li>
<li>Travel to the East Coast of the United States.</li>
<li>We’re happy to<strong>&nbsp;sponsor your AWS certification.</strong></li>
<li>Devote 10% of your working time to innovative projects or education of your choice.</li>
<li>Budget to attend your favorite conferences.</li>
<li>A beautiful office in Karlin, with a full fridge, amazing coffee, a Multisport card to keep in shape, and massages for a good recovery.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>How to apply</strong></p>
<p>Include whatever information you think would help us to understand your background better – whether it is your GitHub, Twitter, blog, or anything else you deem appropriate.</p>
