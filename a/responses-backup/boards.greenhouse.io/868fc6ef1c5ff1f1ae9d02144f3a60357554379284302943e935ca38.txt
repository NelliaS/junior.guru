  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <section>
<p>We are looking for a software engineer with knowledge of OOP to join our team and help develop our cloud-based service, YSoft OMNI Series. The role calls for someone who is a quick learner and happy to get stuck into a challenge. We combine both modern software development techniques (such as fast paced agile development, CI/CD) and a very open, friendly and fail-fast atmosphere. If you’re eager to learn, come along and join the ride!</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>What you’ll do:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Work on the development of YSoft OMNI Series</li>
<li>Test software development in an agile environment</li>
<li>Provide ongoing maintenance, support, and enhancements</li>
<li>Provide recommendations for continuous improvement</li>
<li>Work alongside the team to elevate the service and apply best practices</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Technologies and enviroment in which you will work: </h2>
<p>Must-haves</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with OOP (.NET Core / .NET 5 / Java / C# / Python, JS, PHP...)</li>
</ul>
<p>We are working with, and we will be happy to teach you</p>
<ul>
<li>Azure</li>
<li>DevOps</li>
<li>CI/CD</li>
<li>IoT</li>
<li>OOP</li>
<li>.NET</li>
<li>React</li>
<li>SHELL scripting</li>
<li>QA</li>
<li>Application security</li>
<li>LESS Scrum</li>
<li>Pair programming</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>About you: </h2>
<ul>
<li>You’re a T-shaped person - capable in multiple areas and an expert in one</li>
<li>You can openly share your opinion, take part in discussions, ask questions </li>
<li>You need to manage truthful feedback, both positive and negative</li>
<li>You have a sense of humor </li>
<li>You have willingness to continuously learn and apply what you already know</li>
<li>You have the ability to adapt to dynamically changing environments/tools/processes</li>
<li>If you have a certain area of interest (for example architecture, security, QA), you can participate in one of our Communities of Practice (CoP) </li>
<li>Experience with Scrum / Agile development methodologies</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What you can expect:</strong> </p>
<ul>
<li>A very open and honest environment with a startup feel</li>
<li>Opportunities to continuously learn and apply what you already know </li>
<li>Become part of a self-sufficient team </li>
<li>Great benefits, including 30 days of vacation, a company stock allocation program, and free use of 3D printers </li>
<li>Many internal activities organized by YSofters for YSofters (Y4Y) </li>
<li>Opportunities to stand out from the crowd—if you want to speak at a meetup or organize the next session of our regular lunch &amp; stories, Y Soft will support you! </li>
</ul>

</section>
