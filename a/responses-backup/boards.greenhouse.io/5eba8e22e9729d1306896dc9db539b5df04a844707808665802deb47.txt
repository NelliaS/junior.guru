    <div class="content-intro"><p><strong>DevRev</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">DevRev is ushering in the era of Developer Customer Relationship Management.&nbsp; The foundation of this new category is an end-to-end platform for developers to build products, support end users, and grow their businesses in a product-led era of all things code. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">The outcome will bring </span><strong>Dev</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">elopers closer to </span><strong>Rev</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">enue and increase developer productivity by humanizing software development with the use of design, data engineering, and machine intelligence (AI/ML).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">DevRev is a company for developers built by developers. We at DevRev believe that to efficiently connect developers to customers, we must harness the knowledge of engineers to build a knowledge-centric system, making enterprise software more product- and customer-centric.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">After former Nutanix CEO Dheeraj Pandey and Nutanix executive Manoj Agarwal raised one of the largest ever seed rounds in the history of Silicon Valley, they set out to create a </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">highly engaging system of record that promises to make product development and customer service joyous</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">. DevRev is solving a difficult AI and design problem, and the early employees are committed to the marathon of company-building and hard problem-solving.</span></p></div>

    <p><strong>Job Description</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are looking for ambitious and authentic software developers who are excited to bring fresh ideas from all areas to their work, including artificial intelligence, natural language processing, UI design, mobile, information retrieval, distributed computing, large-scale system design, networking and data storage, security, and more. As a software developer at DevRev, you will have the opportunity to build things that truly matter and to work with a thriving global team.</span></p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work closely with our product and design teams to build new and innovative application experiences for our users</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work closely with backend developers to build highly scalable applications&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Build reusable and responsive components to scale multiple device types</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Optimize UI for the best performance, quality and efficiency</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Minimum Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field, or equivalent practical experience</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience in frontend development, with knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript, React.js, Vue.js, Node.js, and frontend-related technologies</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with latest UI libraries and frameworks</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience in building full-stack web and mobile applications</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience in coding Java or Golang</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with PWA (Progressive Web Apps), Single Page Applications is a bonus</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Knowledge of JavaScript advanced features</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

    <div class="content-conclusion"><p><strong>Culture</strong></p>
<p>The foundation of DevRev is its culture -- our commitment to those who are hungry, humble, honest, and who act with heart. Our vision is to help build the earth’s most customer-centric companies. Our mission is to leverage design, data engineering, and machine intelligence to empower engineers to embrace their customers. That is <strong>DevRev</strong>!</p></div>
