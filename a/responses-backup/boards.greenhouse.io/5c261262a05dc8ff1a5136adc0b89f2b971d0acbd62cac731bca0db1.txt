  <h1 class='app-title'>Python Software Engineer - Exponea</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Bloomreach
  </span>

    <p><strong>We are Bloomreach, </strong>an internationally renowned Digital Experience Platform. We are currently valued at $900M, after a $150M investment and acquisition of Exponea, the #1 CDP on G2crowd. We work with global e-commerce leaders and other top companies from all over the globe.</p>
<p><img src="https://www.bloomreach.com/binaries/original/content/gallery/bloomreach.com_2/blog-images/2021/brex-announcement.jpg" alt="" width="424" height="156"></p>
<p>We are currently expanding our <strong>Engineering team </strong>and are looking for new <strong>Software</strong><strong>&nbsp;engineers</strong>. The salary starts at €2,500. Could you be one of them?</p>
<div class="section global-width">
<div class="block">
<h2>About your role and the team:</h2>
<div class="block-wrapper description">
<p>As part of our team, you will be working on code that allows the Exponea application to reliably ingest large amounts of data as quickly as possible. Your primary focus will be to keep the <span style="font-weight: 400;">real time data ingestion</span> stable, to ensure it can be easily scaled out on demand and to provide visibility to all processing stages. Your daily routine may include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and maintaining our components to be more robust and scalable.</li>
<li>Monitoring and increasing the <span style="font-weight: 400;">real time data ingestion</span> throughput.</li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Maintaining the ingestion APIs.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Collaborating on architecture design.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Participate in 24/7 on-call rotation.</span></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="section global-width">
<div class="block">
<div class="block-wrapper description">
<p><strong>Competencies you already have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">At least 2 years of experience in Python programming.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Good grasp of software development techniques (debugging, testing, code review, etc.)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with basic data structures.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Basic understanding of Docker on a user level.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Interest in GO.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Competencies you will need to acquire:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with the python ecosystem (libraries, frameworks).</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with service oriented architecture with focus on performance and scalability.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with maintaining code in production.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Basic knowledge of monitoring and observability.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with kubernetes on a user level.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with kafka, mongo, redis.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with CI/CD.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Your personal traits:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Willingness to learn.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to clearly communicate.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Willingness to dive into documentation.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Out-of-the-box thinking.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Critical thinking.</span></li>
</ul>
<h2>Our tech stack:</h2>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Python, GO</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Google Cloud Platform</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kubernetes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Apache Kafka</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mongo, Redis</span></li>
<li>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="section global-width">
<div class="block">
<div class="block-wrapper description">
<h2><strong>Regional benefits:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Monthly lunch entitlement by up to 110€ per month</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">Pension Scheme or Health insurance depending on the region</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

    <div class="content-conclusion"><h2 style="text-align: justify;"><strong>More things you'll like about Bloomreach:</strong></h2>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Culture:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">A great deal of freedom and trust. At Bloomreach we don’t clock in and out, and we have neither corporate rules nor long approval processes. This freedom goes hand in hand with responsibility. We are interested in results from day one.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have defined our </span><a href="https://careers.bloomreach.com/about-us"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 values</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and the 10 underlying key behaviors that we strongly believe in. We can only succeed if everyone lives these behaviors day to day. We've embedded them in our processes like recruitment, onboarding, feedback, personal development, performance review and internal communication.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We believe in flexible working hours to accommodate your working style.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We work remote-first with several Bloomreach Hubs available across three continents.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize company events (summits) twice a year to experience the global spirit of the company and get excited about what's ahead.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a friendly atmosphere and motivated colleagues who love what they do.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="https://www.glassdoor.com/Overview/Working-at-Bloomreach-EI_IE442167.11,21.htm"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreach Glassdoor page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> elaborates on our stellar 4.5/5 rating.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Personal Development:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a People Development Program -- participating in weekly personal development workshops on various topics run by experts from inside the company. We are continuously developing &amp; updating competency maps for select functions.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our managers are strongly encouraged to participate in the Leader Development Program to develop in the areas we consider essential for any leader. The program includes regular comprehensive feedback, consultations with a coach and follow-up check-ins.</span></li>

</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Well-being:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Employee Assistance Program -- with counselors -- is available for non-work-related challenges.</span></li>

<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize ‘DisConnect’ days where Bloomreachers globally enjoy one additional day off each quarter, allowing us to unwind together and focus on activities away from the screen with our loved ones.</span></li>
