  <h1 class='app-title'>Python Software Engineer - Exponea</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Bloomreach
  </span>

    <p><strong>We are Bloomreach, </strong>an internationally renowned Digital Experience Platform. We are currently valued at $900M, after a $150M investment and acquisition of Exponea, the #1 CDP on G2crowd. We work with global e-commerce leaders and other top companies from all over the globe.</p>
<p><img src="https://www.bloomreach.com/binaries/original/content/gallery/bloomreach.com_2/blog-images/2021/brex-announcement.jpg" alt="" width="424" height="156"></p>
<p>We are currently expanding our <strong>Engineering team </strong>and are looking for new <strong>Software</strong><strong>&nbsp;engineers</strong>. The salary starts at €2,500. Could you be one of them?</p>
<div class="section global-width">
<div class="block">
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">About your role and the team:</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The purpose of the Data pipeline team is to ingest a </span><strong>large amount of data into our application reliably and at a high rate</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. As a member of our team, you could work on components that need to </span><strong>scale to process tens of thousands of requests per second</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. We scale our microservices horizontally to tens of instances thanks to Kubernetes and Google cloud. Our microservices push data through Kafka and use many storages like Redis and Mongo.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you are passionate about </span><strong>scaling, microservices</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><strong>pushing the performance to the limit</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, and you want to work with </span><strong>distributed systems</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, the Data pipeline team is the correct team for you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your daily routine in our team may include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Developing and maintaining our components to be more reliable, maintainable,&nbsp; and scalable.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Collaborating on the design of distributed, business-critical systems.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working together with DevOps engineers to monitor and improve our components in production.</span></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="section global-width">
<div class="block">
<div class="block-wrapper description">
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">What you might like about this role</span></h2>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">The perks and challenges of our projects:</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have one of the best Customer Data and Experience Platforms (CDXP) in the world. Our clients love what we do, which is reflected in our high Net Promoter Score. You can participate by suggesting new product features and ideas.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our components process tens of thousands of events per second so that our marketing automation, analysis, and web layers can deliver value to the customer in near real-time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">When we work on projects we work together on the solution with product, security, and business to deliver the right solution at the right time. </span><strong>You will have a great impact on what we build and how we build it.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our biggest challenge is our growth. </span><strong>We need to constantly think about how we can make our services robust, scalable, reliable, and at the same time how to keep the maintenance low.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To keep up with the pace, </span><strong>every team member is encouraged to learn continuously</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. That is why one of our bonuses is a very sweet education budget.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">What you can learn with us</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Deepen your familiarity with the Python ecosystem (libraries, frameworks).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Get hands-on experience with service-oriented architecture with a focus on performance and scalability.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">How to maintain code in production deployed in Kubernetes.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Learn more about of monitoring and observability.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our tech stack</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Python, GO</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Google Cloud Platform</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kubernetes</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Apache Kafka</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mongo, Redis</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">What we expect of the candidate</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are looking for an engineer with:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">At least 2 years of experience in Python programming.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Good grasp of software development techniques (debugging, testing, code review, etc.)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with basic data structures.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Basic understanding of Docker on a user level.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Interest in GO.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Willingness to learn and dive into complex problems.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to clearly communicate.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Critical thinking.</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">At least 2 years of experience in Python programming.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Good grasp of software development techniques (debugging, testing, code review, etc.)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with basic data structures.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Basic understanding of Docker on a user level.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Interest in GO.</span></li>
</ul>

    <div class="content-conclusion"><h2>More things you'll like about Bloomreach:</h2>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Culture:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">A great deal of freedom and trust. At Bloomreach we don’t clock in and out, and we have neither corporate rules nor long approval processes. This freedom goes hand in hand with responsibility. We are interested in results from day one.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have defined our </span><a href="https://careers.bloomreach.com/about-us"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 values</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and the 10 underlying key behaviors that we strongly believe in. We can only succeed if everyone lives these behaviors day to day. We've embedded them in our processes like recruitment, onboarding, feedback, personal development, performance review and internal communication.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We believe in flexible working hours to accommodate your working style.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We work remote-first with several Bloomreach Hubs available across three continents.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize company events (summits) twice a year to experience the global spirit of the company and get excited about what's ahead.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a friendly atmosphere and motivated colleagues who love what they do.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="https://www.glassdoor.com/Overview/Working-at-Bloomreach-EI_IE442167.11,21.htm"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreach Glassdoor page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> elaborates on our stellar 4.5/5 rating.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Personal Development:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a People Development Program -- participating in weekly personal development workshops on various topics run by experts from inside the company. We are continuously developing &amp; updating competency maps for select functions.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our managers are strongly encouraged to participate in the Leader Development Program to develop in the areas we consider essential for any leader. The program includes regular comprehensive feedback, consultations with a coach and follow-up check-ins.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreachers utilize the $1,500 professional education budget on an annual basis to purchase education products (books, courses, certifications, etc.)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our resident communication coach </span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/ivo-ve%C4%8De%C5%99a-2a94291/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ivo Večeřa</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is available to help navigate work-related communications &amp; decision-making challenges.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Well-being:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Employee Assistance Program -- with counselors -- is available for non-work-related challenges.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Subscription to Calm - sleep and meditation app.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize ‘DisConnect’ days where Bloomreachers globally enjoy one additional day off each quarter, allowing us to unwind together and focus on activities away from the screen with our loved ones.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We facilitate sports, yoga, and meditation opportunities for each other.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Compensation:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Stock options are granted depending on a team member’s role, seniority, and location.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Everyone gets to participate in the company's success through the company performance bonus.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We offer an employee referral bonus of up to $3,000 paid out immediately after the new hire starts.</span></li>
