    <h3><strong>Job Overview</strong></h3>
<p>The eCommerce Coordinator plays a key role in the operation of the Wargaming Store, involved in bringing new World of Tanks merchandise to market, planning and organizing the marketing campaigns, and analyzing the results. The position provides capacity for growth in a fun, dynamic and multicultural environment</p>
<h3><strong>Reports To</strong></h3>
<p>The eCommerce Coordinator will report to the Head of eCommerce &amp; Partnerships</p>
<h3><strong>What will you do?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with eShop partner to bring new products to market in a timely manner that meets market requirements (pricing, quality, season, etc.);</li>
<li>Construct promotional and communication plans &amp; activities on a monthly basis for the eShop in coordination with partners and internal stakeholders;</li>
<li>Execute monthly marketing campaigns with eShop partner and internal teams</li>
<li>Recommend the most appropriate communication channel, and messaging for promoting products and eShop activities</li>
<li>Perform weekly, monthly and quarterly reports in additional to ad hoc reports</li>
<li>Create all related JIRA tasks for product development, marketing campaigns and communication plans</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What are we looking for?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Junior candidate with at least one year of experience in eCommerce</li>
<li>Fluent in English (Native Speaker preferred)</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel, PowerPoint, Google Analytics</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Agile</li>
<li>Results oriented</li>
<li>Ability to manage stress</li>
<li>Rigorous</li>
<li>Comfortable with data</li>
<li>Attention to details</li>
<li>Creative</li>
<li>Team Player</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What additional skills will help you stand out?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Shopify would be a plus</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>About Wargaming:</strong></h3>
<p>Wargaming is an award-winning online game developer and publisher headquartered in Nicosia, Cyprus. Delivering legendary games since 1998, Wargaming has grown to become one of the leaders in the free-to-play MMO gaming industry with 4500+ employees and more than 20 offices globally. Over 200 million players enjoy Wargaming’s titles across all major gaming platforms, including the massively popular World of Tanks and World of Warships.</p>
<p>Working in our company means always having interesting challenges and gaining valuable experience while working with top-class experts.</p>
<p>Take your place among our passionate and experienced team and bring out the best in yourself at Wargaming!</p>
