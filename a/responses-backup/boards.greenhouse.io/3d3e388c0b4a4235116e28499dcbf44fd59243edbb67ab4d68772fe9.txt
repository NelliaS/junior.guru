  <h1 class='app-title'>FullStack Engineer</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Y Soft

  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <section>
<p>One of our cross-functional teams is looking for a Full Stack Engineer to join our global company. We are looking for someone to support the development of YSoft OMNI Series, our ground-breaking technology that connects any in-market network printer or multifunction printer to the cloud.</p>
<p>The Y Soft R&amp;D department includes over 100 professionals working in a manager-less team. We share one product backlog and are self-organized. At Y Soft, you’ll get the opportunity to collaborate with other experienced developers, learning as you go.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Technologies with which you will work:</h2>
<p>We work with a wide and diverse range of technologies. Knowledge of all of them is not necessary, we are happy to teach you, but knowledge of the following core technology is necessary.</p>
<p>Core knowledge:</p>
<ul>
<li>C#</li>
<li>React</li>
<li>Docker (at least basic knowledge is required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Other technology we work with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Front end
<ul>
<li>Typescript, (React Hooks, functional based components, useSWR, axios)</li>
<li>Fluent UI design system</li>
<li>Prettier, ESLint</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Backend, API
<ul>
<li>.NET Core, .NET 5</li>
<li>Azure functions</li>
<li>REST APIs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Automatic tests
<ul>
<li>NUnit, Specflow</li>
<li>Cypress, Selenium</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>CI/CD
<ul>
<li>Bamboo</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Azure services
<ul>
<li>App Service, Key Vault, Storage Account (Table, Queue), Azure AD</li>
<li>IoT Hub, IoT Edge Devices</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>IDEs
<ul>
<li>Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, Rider, IntelliJ IDEA</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Job responsibilities:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the development of cloud-based solutions</li>
<li>Work on different components of OMNI Series - both backend and frontend</li>
<li>If you have a certain area of interest for example architecture, security, or QA, you can take part in our Communities of Practice (CoP)</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>What we expect from you:</h2>
<ul>
<li>T-Shaped approach – a wide knowledge of many things and an expert in one</li>
<li>Knowledge of C#, React and Docker – anything else is a bonus</li>
<li>Active involvement in team activities and decision-making</li>
<li>Ability to openly share your opinion, take part in discussions, and ask questions</li>
<li>Good communications skills in both English and Czech</li>
<li>Sense of humor</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You can look forward to:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Generous benefits, including 30 days of vacation, company stock allocation program, and free use of 3D printers</li>
<li>Being part of changing the processes and environment at Y Soft</li>
<li>Many internal activities organized by YSofters for YSofters (Y4Y)</li>
<li>The opportunity to stand out from the crowd, such as being a speaker, contributor, or organizer for an event, Y Soft will support you</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</section>
