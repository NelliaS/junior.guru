  <span class='company-name'>
    at Canonical - Jobs

  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Combine your Python software engineering skills, your love of travel, and your general knowledge of the full stack, from hardware to application, networking, Linux and open source, to help customers embrace Canonical's offering on public and private clouds. Our field engineering team bring infrastructure-as-code and high-level application automation to bear to help customers create sophisticated open source deployments - from OpenStack to Kubernetes, Hadoop to Airflow.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This is an exceptional opportunity for a competitive open source technology leader who enjoys solving problems under pressure and wants to travel regularly. The main responsibilities of this position are:&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Apply your knowledge of Linux, virtualisation using libvirt/KVM and your knowledge of TCP/IP networking to deliver customised solutions for our customers.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Understanding and hands on experience with popular open source software such as PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Kafka, Cassandra and NGINX.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Discuss detailed architecture, designing complex solutions and guiding customers to successful data-center infrastructure operations</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Engage early on in the pre-sales process and remain engaged throughout the delivery phase</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you have a passion for the latest open source technologies, cloud-native development, data-center technologies such as Openstack, Kubernetes, Public Cloud (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and computer networking, you will love the Field Engineer role at Canonical.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>The successful Field Software Engineer will have :</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">BA/BS degree in Computer Science or related technical field</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">3 years of Linux development experience</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Must be able to speak English fluently</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strong background contributing code in Python and Go to open source projects in the cloud or devices area</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Excellent communication and presentation skills</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">High motivation, ability to multi-task and follow-up reliably on commitments</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Interest in customer-facing engagement, including pitching, demonstrating and understanding customer environment and needs</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience working with at least one or more of these technologies OpenStack, Kubernetes, Ceph, and/or Ubuntu</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with or willingness to learn new technologies, including LXD, Snaps, MAAS, Juju and Ubuntu</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to travel globally for periods up to two weeks; up to 50% of travel time (pending on covid-19 restrictions)</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Second language - French, German, Italian or Spanish</span></li>
</ul>

We are committed to ensuring equal employment opportunities to all qualified individuals. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, national origin, genetics, disability, age, or veteran status.&nbsp;</span></em></p>
