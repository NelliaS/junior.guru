  <h1 class='app-title'>Software Engineer (Recommendation team)</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Bloomreach
  </span>

    <a data-mapped="true" href="/bloomreach">(View all jobs)</a>

    <div class='location'>
      Bratislava, Brno, Prague, Remote Central and Eastern Europe
    </div>
</div>
  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <p><strong>We are Bloomreach, </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">an internationally renowned Digital Experience Platform. We are currently valued at $900M, after a $150M investment and acquisition of Exponea, the #1 CDP on G2crowd. We work with global e-commerce leaders and other top companies from all over the globe.</span></p>
<p><img src="https://www.bloomreach.com/binaries/original/content/gallery/bloomreach.com_2/blog-images/2021/brex-announcement.jpg" alt="" width="424" height="156"></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are currently expanding our </span><strong>Recommendations Team</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and are looking for a new colleague as a <strong>Software</strong></span><strong> Engineer</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. The salary range is €2,500 - €3,500. Could you be the one we're looking for?</span></p>
<h2><strong>About your role and the team:</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The goal of the Recommendations team is to improve customer experience through </span><strong>personalized recommendations and predictions</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. It allows online stores to better connect with their customers, and customers to find relevant products out of thousands available. As a Software Engineer, you will work with large amounts of customer behaviour data to build and maintain:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Machine learning-based product recommendation platform for hundreds of e-commerce stores serving millions of customers.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Predictive analytics to learn customers behaviour and predict their future actions.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Next-gen A/B/* testing based on contextual multi-armed bandit models.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>We are looking for an engineer with an affinity for data science.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Most of the time you’ll use your engineering skills. Occasionally you’ll tackle machine learning models to make our predictions and recommendations even better. Your tasks and responsibilities will be to:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Maintain ML models</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> that are used by hundreds of our e-commerce clients. Each of our clients uses their model in different ways, with different kinds of data about customers and their behaviour. It’s our job to make the models versatile and foolproof.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Build and maintain APIs for recommendations and predictions.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Our product has a lot of various features that consume the machine learning models through their APIs.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Design and build data pipelines and storage</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for ML models. Data engineering is an important part of this job, as we maintain our models ourselves. That means robust high-throughput pipelines, fast data storage for real-time outputs, reliable scalable infrastructure, and quality monitoring and alerting.</span></p>
<h2><strong>What you might like about this role:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>The perks and challenges of our Recommendations Team projects:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have one of the best Customer Data and Experience Platforms (CDXP) in the world. </span><strong>Our clients love what we do,</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> which is reflected in our high Net Promoter Score. You can participate by suggesting new product features and ideas.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Every hour, our models recommend the next product to buy to millions of people around the globe. </span><strong>The impact of your work will be huge.</strong></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our recommendations and predictions are served on-demand in near-real time. </span><strong>Speed is critical for us</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, and represents one of the biggest challenges for the team. If increasing data throughput and decreasing response times is your thing, this job is for you.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>We grow fast</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and our systems need to grow with us. We rewrite our pipeline often so that it’s more robust and scalable.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">This job is demanding and requires continuous learning. That’s why every team member is granted a </span><strong>yearly Udemy budget</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to advance in their proficiency.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our tech stack:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Python (NumPy, SciKit) &amp; Go</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Google BigQuery</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">SQL, MongoDB, Redis, Elasticsearch</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Apache Kafka</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kubernetes, Prometheus</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">various Google Cloud Platform services (GCS, pub/sub, ...)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Gitlab CI/CD</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What we expect of the candidate:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Must have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Senior-level programming skill, ability to write clean and modular code.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Solid experience in building HTTP and gRPC APIs.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience delivering high-throughput distributed infrastructure.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with storage and database technologies including relational and NoSQL databases.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to collaborate in a cross-functional team using strong written and verbal communication.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with Python, Go or similar language.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with data science and machine learning</span><strong> is optional.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> It’s OK if you are a software developer with no prior experience with this stuff - we will provide you with opportunities to learn.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with running data-intensive applications (batch or real-time processing).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to compare pros and cons of storage or database solutions and pick the best fit for a particular problem.</span></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: right;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">#LI-DU1</span></p>

    <div class="content-conclusion"><h2>More things you'll like about Bloomreach:</h2>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Culture:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">A great deal of freedom and trust. At Bloomreach we don’t clock in and out, and we have neither corporate rules nor long approval processes. This freedom goes hand in hand with responsibility. We are interested in results from day one.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have defined our </span><a href="https://careers.bloomreach.com/about-us"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 values</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and the 10 underlying key behaviors that we strongly believe in. We can only succeed if everyone lives these behaviors day to day. We've embedded them in our processes like recruitment, onboarding, feedback, personal development, performance review and internal communication.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We believe in flexible working hours to accommodate your working style.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We work remote-first with several Bloomreach Hubs available across three continents.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize company events (summits) twice a year to experience the global spirit of the company and get excited about what's ahead.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a friendly atmosphere and motivated colleagues who love what they do.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="https://www.glassdoor.com/Overview/Working-at-Bloomreach-EI_IE442167.11,21.htm"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreach Glassdoor page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> elaborates on our stellar 4.5/5 rating.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Personal Development:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a People Development Program -- participating in weekly personal development workshops on various topics run by experts from inside the company. We are continuously developing &amp; updating competency maps for select functions.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our managers are strongly encouraged to participate in the Leader Development Program to develop in the areas we consider essential for any leader. The program includes regular comprehensive feedback, consultations with a coach and follow-up check-ins.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreachers utilize the $1,500 professional education budget on an annual basis to purchase education products (books, courses, certifications, etc.)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our resident communication coach </span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/ivo-ve%C4%8De%C5%99a-2a94291/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ivo Večeřa</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is available to help navigate work-related communications &amp; decision-making challenges.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Well-being:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Employee Assistance Program -- with counselors -- is available for non-work-related challenges.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Subscription to Calm - sleep and meditation app.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize ‘DisConnect’ days where Bloomreachers globally enjoy one additional day off each quarter, allowing us to unwind together and focus on activities away from the screen with our loved ones.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We facilitate sports, yoga, and meditation opportunities for each other.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Compensation:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Stock options are granted depending on a team member’s role, seniority, and location.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Everyone gets to participate in the company's success through the company performance bonus.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We offer an employee referral bonus of up to $3,000 paid out immediately after the new hire starts.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We celebrate work anniversaries -- Bloomversaries!</span></li>
</ul>
