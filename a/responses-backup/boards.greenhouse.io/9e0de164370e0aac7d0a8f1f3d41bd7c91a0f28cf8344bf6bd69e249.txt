    <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At the end of 2020, we launched a new product Wandera Private Access (WPA), which allows companies to securely access their applications and data stored in the cloud. We are looking for an </span><strong>experienced C#/.NET Developer</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to join our growing Windows Team.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Last year, our Win10 customer base expanded by more than 400% and we expect similar growth even this year. Together we are building the best product for remote working based on the principles of the Zero Trust Network.</span></p>
<p><strong>Good things aren't the easy ones üìà&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will gain end to end experience developing our new Windows 10 client. That will include working on areas ranging from deep in the Windows OS networking stack, to the app‚Äôs core business logic, to the backend RESTful API integration and also UI development. We experienced that the Windows 10 platform brought us a lot of new technological challenges and a new set of customer expectations. There is plenty of development ahead.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a developer, you will become a part of a small team and get plenty of input and ownership in the design, delivery and release of our software.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Skills pay bills ‚≠ê</strong></p>
<p><strong>To&nbsp; succeed in this position, you will be required:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">C# and .NET Software Development - both Win32 and WPF</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Understanding of network protocols (IP, TCP/UDP, DNS)</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br></span><strong>Strong bonus, if you have experience with:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Windows Filtering Platform (WFP)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Network Driver Interface Specification (NDIS)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Akka.net framework</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">C++ (for low-level networking)</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br></span><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with Windows Communication Foundation (WCF)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with NUnit framework for unit tests&nbsp;</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Overall, we are looking for a Windows developer with the necessary technical aptitude, enthusiasm and collaboration skills required to thrive at Wandera.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Clean code.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working on </span><a href="https://techspective.net/2020/12/01/wandera-launches-wandera-private-access-for-secure-remote-connectivity/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">top security product</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> used world-wide.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Flexible working hours (whether you are finishing your diploma thesis or picking up children from kindergarten).</span></li>
