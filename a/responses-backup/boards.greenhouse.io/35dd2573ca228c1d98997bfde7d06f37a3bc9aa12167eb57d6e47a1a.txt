  <h1 class='app-title'>Junior Java Software Engineer</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Y Soft
  </span>

  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <section>
<p>If you’re a junior Java developer thrilled/ready to work on a self-managed team, we want to talk to you! </p>
<p>One of our cross-functional software engineering teams of seven developers is looking for a Junior Java Software Engineer. In this role, you will take part in the development of our main product, YSoft SAFEQ. Your team might be small, but the department you will be joining is not; our Research &amp; Development (R&amp;D) department has over 100 professionals. We work in manager-less teams supported by agile coaches and we share one product backlog.   </p>
</section>
<section>
<h2> Technologies and enviroment you’ll work with: </h2>
<ul>
<li>Java11+, Spring 5+, Gradle, Git, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, Helm, Terraform</li>
<li>Manager-less teams supported by agile coaches</li>
<li>LeSS framework</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>What you’ll do: </h2>
<ul>
<li>Work on different components of our main product, YSoft SAFEQ  </li>
<li>Take part in team activities such as planning, refinement, and retrospectives </li>
<li>Participate in one of our Communities of Practice (CoP), if interested  </li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>About you: </h2>
<ul>
<li>You’re a T-shaped person - capable in multiple areas, expert in one of them </li>
<li>You have good knowledge of Java a willingness to learn other technologies </li>
<li>It’s a plus if you have some experience with Azure, React, TypeScript, Kubernetes, C#, .NET, .NET core, Docker, and other technologies </li>
<li>You can openly share your opinion even outside your team, take part in discussions, ask questions </li>
<li>You have a sense of humor </li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You can look forward to: </strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Opportunities to continuously learn and apply what you already know </li>
<li>Becoming part of a self-sufficient team </li>
<li>Great benefits, including 30 days of vacation, a company stock allocation program, and free use of 3D printes </li>
<li>Many internal activities organized by YSofters for YSofters (Y4Y) </li>
<li>Opportunities to stand out from the crowd—if you want to speak at a meetup or organize the next session of our regular lunch &amp; stories, Y Soft will support you! </li>
</ul>
</section>
