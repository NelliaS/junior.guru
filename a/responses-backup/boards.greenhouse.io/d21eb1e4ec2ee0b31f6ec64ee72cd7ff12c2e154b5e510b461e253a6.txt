  <h1 class='app-title'>Software Engineer - Golang (Web Experience team)</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Bloomreach
  </span>

    <a data-mapped="true" href="/bloomreach">(View all jobs)</a>

    <div class='location'>
      Bratislava, Brno, Prague
    </div>
</div>
  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Become a </span><strong>Software Engineer</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for Bloomreach! Join our </span><strong>Web Experience team </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">and</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">help us develop and maintain components written in </span><strong>Golang</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Your work will impact hundreds of millions of customers in the online space. Your salary starts at <strong>2300</strong></span><strong>€ per month</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> with stock options and other benefits included. Working in one of our </span><strong>Central Europe offices or from home</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> on a </span><strong>full-time basis</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, you´ll become a part of the </span><strong>Engineering Team</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<h2><strong>What tech stack do we have for you?</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Golang</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jinja template engine (and our own implementation in Golang)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">gRPC</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kubernetes in combination with Google Cloud</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Gitlab CI</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Your job will be to:</strong></h2>
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>&nbsp;Deliver new features</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cooperate on design and solution together with both technical and product-oriented colleagues</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Development of new features with focus on quality and performance</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Maintain and Improve build and deploy pipelines in Gitlab CI</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Prototyping of new ideas<br><br></span><strong>Ensure quality and performance of developed features</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Write high-performance Golang code</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Measure and tune performance of our APIs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Make sure we do collect and monitor performance metrics<br><br></span><strong>Support and Maintain owned components</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Support our client facing colleagues in the investigation of possible issues (L3 support)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fixing of found issues on a regular basis</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>About your role and the team:</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Web Experience team is responsible for multiple components - API (in Golang), client-side SDK (in TypeScript) and browser extension (in JS/Vue.js). All these components together are delivering the client-side personalisation to the websites of our customers.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your primary focus will be on the API services, where you will be developing new features and maintaining the codebase to ensure quality. We do care about performance not only on the level of one system (code optimisation), but also in the area of intersystem communication, that is why we use gRPC, event streams, messaging queue and fast NoSQL databases.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We need to grant reliability and availability of our SaaS product. Therefore we invest in monitoring and alerting systems. The team is built with E2E ownership, DevOps principles and an Agile mindset - so you will be participating in all parts of the product development cycle - from design &amp; architecture to maintenance, operations and monitoring of our systems.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Web experience team also owns components written in TypeScript/JavaScript - client-side SDK and browser extension. So you may also dig in this area and get new knowledge if you want to, of course with support from more senior colleagues in this area.</span></p>
<h2><strong>You have these qualities and experience:</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Professional</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Knowledge of Golang </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">if you are junior in Golang you may still amaze us with knowledge of other scripting language and ability to learn quickly</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes and other DevOps tooling</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience with CI/CD approach and ideally Gitlab CI</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Knowledge of gRPC</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experience in area of monitoring and alerting of cloud based services -</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Prometheus, Grafana, Sentry</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Personal</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ability to work in teams effectively and reliably</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Willingness to learn new things and develop your skillset</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">A “can-do” attitude, Ability to solve problems proactively</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Fluency in Englis</span></li>
</ul>

    <div class="content-conclusion"><h2>More things you'll like about Bloomreach:</h2>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Culture:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">A great deal of freedom and trust. At Bloomreach we don’t clock in and out, and we have neither corporate rules nor long approval processes. This freedom goes hand in hand with responsibility. We are interested in results from day one.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have defined our </span><a href="https://careers.bloomreach.com/about-us"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5 values</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and the 10 underlying key behaviors that we strongly believe in. We can only succeed if everyone lives these behaviors day to day. We've embedded them in our processes like recruitment, onboarding, feedback, personal development, performance review and internal communication.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We believe in flexible working hours to accommodate your working style.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We work remote-first with several Bloomreach Hubs available across three continents.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize company events (summits) twice a year to experience the global spirit of the company and get excited about what's ahead.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a friendly atmosphere and motivated colleagues who love what they do.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The </span><a href="https://www.glassdoor.com/Overview/Working-at-Bloomreach-EI_IE442167.11,21.htm"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreach Glassdoor page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> elaborates on our stellar 4.5/5 rating.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Personal Development:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We have a People Development Program -- participating in weekly personal development workshops on various topics run by experts from inside the company. We are continuously developing &amp; updating competency maps for select functions.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our managers are strongly encouraged to participate in the Leader Development Program to develop in the areas we consider essential for any leader. The program includes regular comprehensive feedback, consultations with a coach and follow-up check-ins.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bloomreachers utilize the $1,500 professional education budget on an annual basis to purchase education products (books, courses, certifications, etc.)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our resident communication coach </span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/ivo-ve%C4%8De%C5%99a-2a94291/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ivo Večeřa</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is available to help navigate work-related communications &amp; decision-making challenges.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Well-being:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Employee Assistance Program -- with counselors -- is available for non-work-related challenges.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Subscription to Calm - sleep and meditation app.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We organize ‘DisConnect’ days where Bloomreachers globally enjoy one additional day off each quarter, allowing us to unwind together and focus on activities away from the screen with our loved ones.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We facilitate sports, yoga, and meditation opportunities for each other.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Compensation:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Stock options are granted depending on a team member’s role, seniority, and location.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Everyone gets to participate in the company's success through the company performance bonus.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We offer an employee referral bonus of up to $3,000 paid out immediately after the new hire starts.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">We celebrate work anniversaries -- Bloomversaries!</span></li>
</ul>
