  <h1 class='app-title'>Cloud Software Developer</h1>

  <span class='company-name'>
    at Y Soft
  </span>

  <div id="content" class="">
    

    <section>
<p>Our cross-functional team is looking for a&nbsp;new&nbsp;colleague, a&nbsp;Cloud Software&nbsp;Engineer. Y Soft’s&nbsp;Research &amp; Development (R&amp;D) department includes 100+ professionals. We work in manager-less teams supported by agile coaches and we share one product backlog. At Y Soft, you’ll get an opportunity to look at the bigger picture of how our product works, learning all the specifics of its architecture. That’s why you’ll never find yourself working on isolated chunks of a product.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Technologies and the environment in which you will work:&nbsp;</h2>
<ul>
<li>Must: Azure, Kubernetes, Docker, Helm (or similar technologies) and be able to share your knowledge with others&nbsp;</li>
<li>Nice to have: Java or .NET or QA (absence is not a blocker if there is willingness to learn)&nbsp;</li>
<li>Our teams have neither project managers nor people managers—we are truly self-organized&nbsp;</li>
<li>We,&nbsp;as a team,&nbsp;enjoy spending time together - grabbing&nbsp;a beer, laughing&nbsp;over silly jokes and having&nbsp;fun. And,&nbsp;we hope you´ll join us.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Job responsibilities + what we expect:&nbsp;</h2>
<ul>
<li>Participate on any task in the product lifecycle&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ability to share your knowledge about the cloud with others&nbsp;</li>
<li>Willingness to "get your hands dirty"</li>
<li>Take part in team activities such as planning, refinement, and retrospectives</li>
<li>If you have a certain area of interest (e.g., architecture, security, QA), you can participate in one of our Communities of Practice (CoP))</li>
<li>Ability to openly share your opinion, take part in a discussion, ask questions, etc.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</section>
<section>
<h2>You can look forward to:&nbsp;</h2>
<ul>
<li>Opportunity to learn and use what you know in practice&nbsp;</li>
<li>Team of skillful developers with different specializations from whom you can learn a lot from -&gt; Java, C#, QA&nbsp;</li>
<li>Great benefits, including 30 days of vacation, company stock allocation program, and free use of 3D printers</li>
<li>Many internal activities organized by YSofters for YSofters (Y4Y)&nbsp;</li>
<li>Opportunity to stand out from the crowd—no matter if you want to speak at a meetup or organize the next session of our regular lunch &amp; stories, Y Soft will support you!&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</section>
