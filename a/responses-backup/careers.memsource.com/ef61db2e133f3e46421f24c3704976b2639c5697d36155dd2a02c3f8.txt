          <h2>QA Tester / Test Analyst</h2><p>At Memsource we help our customers go global. We provide a leading translation management system (Memsource) and software localization platform (Phrase), supporting 500+ languages, 50+ file types and covering the full range of enterprise localization use cases. With a global team working from our offices or from home across Europe, US, UK and Japan, we serve thousands of global customers, including leading brands such as Uber, Bosch, Lufthansa, Supercell, Vistaprint and Zendesk.</p><p>As a QA Tester/Test Analyst you’ll become an integral part of our growing development team and ensure the highest quality for Memsource products.<br><span class="c2"></span></p><h4 class="c3" id="h.dj7k4kp4wcsj">What you’ll be responsible for:</h4><ul>
<li>Quality Assurance and testing of Memsource cloud-based, desktop, and mobile applications</li>
<li>Creating detailed, comprehensive, and well-structured testing plans and test cases</li>
<li>Identifying, reporting, and retesting bugs</li>
<li>Working independently in a small cross-department team consisting of 1 product manager, 4-6 developers, and 1-2 QA testers</li>
<li>Taking an active part in our agile ceremonies</li>
</ul><p class="c1"><span class="c2"></span></p><h4 class="c3" id="h.x90uc4vtf0up">What you need:</h4><ul>
<li>2+ years experience in manual functional and integration software testing and test analysis</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, attention to detail</li>
<li>Knowledge of SQL, API testing in Postman or SoapUI</li>
<li>Curiosity and passion for learning new things</li>
<li>Willingness to share knowledge with your colleagues</li>
<li>Excellent English skills, Czech is a plus</li>
</ul><p> </p><h4>What you’ll get:</h4><ul>
<li>Work experience in a successful and growing global SaaS company</li>
<li>Be part of an international team of 200+ in Europe, Japan, and the Americas</li>
<li>Expert colleagues in their field who are determined to build the best localization platform on the market</li>
<li>An agile work environment, where it is encouraged to take smart risks</li>
<li>The chance to take responsibility in your role and proactively shape how things are done</li>
<li>Take part in a culture full of trust, support and loyalty, where respectful and open feedback is valued</li>
<li>A positive, open-minded, and innovative atmosphere</li>
<li>Support in your professional development and personal career goals</li>
</ul><h4><br></h4><h4>What's on top:</h4><ul>
<li>Modern technical setup and latest software</li>
<li>Professional and extensive onboarding</li>
<li>Further education through online language classes, conferences, and more</li>
<li>Flexible working hours and home office options</li>
<li>Regularly team building events, such as a summer camping trip and a winter hiking trip</li>
<li>Healthy snacks, coffee, and drinks in our modern, central offices in Prague and Hamburg</li>
</ul><h4><br></h4><p style="font-size:14px;">Memsource is an equal opportunity employer.
