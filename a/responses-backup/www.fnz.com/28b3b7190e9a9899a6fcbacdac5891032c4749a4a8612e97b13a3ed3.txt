							<h1>Careers</h1>
							<p class="lead">FNZ empowers the world’s largest financial institutions to help people achieve their financial goals. We leverage advanced technology and processing scale to deliver improved engagement, seamless service, lower costs and personalisation in financial services.</p>
						</div>
					  </div>
							
					 <div class="dividing-line top-dl"></div>

					  <div class="container content-area">
					  	<div class="col-xs-12 col-md-10 col-md-offset-1">
							<p>As a rapidly growing business we offer an incredible range of opportunities to develop your career at FNZ across a wide range of disciplines:</p>
<ul>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">Our </span><strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">technology teams</strong><span style="line-height: 1.5em;"> help us build and evolve some of the most highly-scaled end-to-end wealth management solutions, using both software and infrastructure engineering. We use highly automated straight-through-processing to manage hundreds of millions of financial transactions every year for millions of investors.</span></li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">OUR&nbsp;<strong>PEOPLE</strong> ARE VITAL IN HELPING TO&nbsp;<strong>CREATE VALUE&nbsp;</strong>FOR OUR CUSTOMERS, THEIR CLIENTS AND OUR BUSINESS</span></p>
</blockquote>
<ul>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">Our </span><strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">asset servicing and support</strong><span style="line-height: 1.5em;"> teams continually focus on delivering an ever more efficient process as well as troubleshooting complex financial transactions, involving high quality service relationships with major market counterparties, investors and intermediaries.</span></li>
<li><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">Our </span><strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">control and risk management </strong><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">teams help us with our awesome responsibility to safeguard the assets and personal data of millions of investors.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>FNZ provides an opportunity to gain unparalleled experience working for an organisation with a unique combination of both retail technology and financial services at one of the fastest and most successful companies in the global FinTech sector.</p>
<p>With a global focus, there are many opportunities to work and travel internationally with a growing number of offices based in Europe, Asia Pacific, North America and Africa.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
