          <h2 class="title">
            Frontend Developer
          </h2>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job description
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <p>At <strong>Monstarlab</strong>, we are in the business of engineering awesome. Relying on stunning design and cutting-edge technology, we never compromise on the methodologies that ensure the delivery of successful, robust, reliable digital solutions and business transformations. With offices in London, Berlin, Aarhus, Prague, Dubai, and Copenhagen, we are one of EMEA’s leading digital product development agencies and, since 2017, a proud part of the global <strong>Monstar-Lab Group</strong>, extending the family to 27 cities in 16 countries.</p>
<p><br></p>
<figure style="display:block;margin:0px;padding:0px;clear:both;font-family:Roboto , sans-serif;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;letter-spacing:normal;text-align:start;text-indent:0px;white-space:normal;color:rgb(51, 51, 51);font-size:14px;background-color:rgb(255, 255, 255);max-width:803px;width:100%;"><img src="https://recruitee-main.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/uploads/17planhamr/Nodes_Office_environment_3_of_8.jpg" style="border:0px;max-width:803px;vertical-align:middle;width:100%;height:auto !important;"></figure>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Monstarlab Prague</strong> is one of the newest addition to our European office network, established with a dedication to creating new services for the agency and a vision to become one of the most important players in the field of advanced digital products and innovation projects in the Czech Republic.</p>
<div>We are looking for a motivated and talented <strong>Frontend Developer </strong>with a passion for learning and growing with a positive, proactive attitude. Monstarlab already creates awesome mobile solutions and we’re building a team that can take that to the web. If you are someone who is comfortable taking an idea from design through to deployment then we would love to hear from you.</div>
<div><strong><br></strong></div>
<div>
<strong>Responsibilities</strong><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Take web builds from design through to final delivery.</li>
<li>Support in the maintenance of web projects after delivery.</li>
<li>Ensure all code is developed using industry-recognized standards</li>
<li>Assist the team with peer reviews and in ensuring the highest quality code is delivered.</li>
<li>Communicate with the design team to ensure designs are well understood, pragmatically thought through, technically feasible and accurately developed</li>
<li>Be prepared to attend client meetings when required</li>
<li>Work with our in-house designers, developers, solution architects and digital consultants.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<strong>Daily tasks:</strong><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Write clean, reusable code and have a passion for web development</li>
<li>Collaborate with clients and first-level support team</li>
<li>Partner closely with front-end, back-end, Android, iOS, PMs and UX team members</li>
<li>Participate in daily stand-ups, sprint planning and estimation</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><strong>Benefits:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>An interesting work environment with talented colleagues</li>
<li>A full-time position with numerous possibilities for growing internally</li>
<li>Office in the beautiful city center of Prague</li>
<li>Regular team nights out, yearly whole-company retreat in another country</li>
<li>Newest Macbook or ThinkPad, Bose headphones, displays, and other hardware</li>
<li>Fully paid unlimited sick days and 4 weeks vacation</li>
<li>Everyday lunches from the best Prague's restaurants</li>
<li>Company provided breakfast, snacks, and all kinds of drinks</li>
<li>Sportscard (Multisport) program</li>
<li>Shared bikes program</li>
<li>Flexible schedule</li>
</ul>
</div>
          </div>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Requirements
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <div>
<strong>It’s important you have the following:</strong><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Experience with a modern JavaScript framework -e.g. React, Angular or VueSolid</li>
<li>Experience with HTML / CSS / Sass and associated tooling</li>
<li> Typescript/NodeJS experience</li>
<li>Experience building, maintaining and iterating on web applications</li>
<li>Experience interfacing with a variety of backend APIs</li>
<li>Experience with a recognised development approach, including both development and test strategies</li>
<li>Experience with modern build pipeline tooling such as Yarn, NPM, Webpack, Bower</li>
<li>2+ years of front-end web application development experience</li>
<li>Experience with any other development technologies or platforms would be beneficial (PHP, C#, Swift, Kotlin, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience working in an agile team</li>
</ul>
