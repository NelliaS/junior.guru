<h2>About the job</h2> <p>The Red Hat Performance Co-Pilot team is looking for an Associate Software Engineer to join us in Brno, Czech Republic. In this role, you will work closely with the rest of the Performance Co-PIlot team on PCP, a system performance analysis toolkit. PCP collects and analyzes systems' performance metrics in real-time or using historical data. You’ll also work on Grafana, which will allow you to query, visualize, alert on, and understand your system’s metrics. You will work as a part of the Performance Co-Pilot team, developing new features and contributing to upstream projects. As an Associate Software Engineer, you'll need to have excellent technical and communication skills because you'll work internally with developers within Red Hat and externally with developers from the open source community.</p> <h2>What you will do</h2> <ul> <li>Respond to requests for enhancement (RFE) from upstream communities or customers by developing new features</li> <li>Work with the Quality Engineering (QE) team to ensure that projects are tested correctly</li> <li>Work on second-level bug reports, identify bugs, and get them fixed</li> <li>Contribute to upstream projects</li> <li>Submit and review patches for bug fixes to and from the community</li> <li>Educate the team on new features via small team presentations</li> </ul> <h2>What you will bring</h2> <ul> <li>Software development experience</li> <li>Knowledge of and experience with C, C++, and Python</li> <li>Passion for or interest in open source development</li> <li>Good communication skills, sense of accountability, and willingness to learn</li> <li>Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field, or equivalent working experience are a plus</li> </ul> <h2>About Red Hat</h2> <p>Red Hat is the world’s leading provider of enterprise open source software solutions, using a community-powered approach to deliver reliable and high-performing Linux, hybrid cloud, container, and Kubernetes technologies. Red Hat helps customers integrate new and existing IT applications, develop cloud-native applications, standardize on our industry-leading operating system, and automate, secure, and manage complex environments. Award-winning support, training, and consulting services make Red Hat a trusted adviser to the Fortune 500. As a strategic partner to cloud providers, system integrators, application vendors, customers, and open source communities, Red Hat can help organizations prepare for the digital future.</p> <p> </p> <p> </p><br><p><i>By submitting your interest in this job, you agree to receive text notifications with additional steps to complete your job application.
