<h2>About the job</h2> <p>The Red Hat LLVM team is looking for a Software Engineer to join us in Brno, Czech Republic. In this role, you will work closely with the rest of the team on the LLDB debugger and LLVM compiler toolchain. You will maintain and help develop the LLDB package in Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) through contributions to the upstream community LLDB project. With support from more experienced engineers on the team, you will develop new features, fix bugs, and resolve security issues that arise. As a Software Engineer, you'll need to have solid technical and communication skills because you'll work internally with developers within Red Hat and externally with developers from the open source community.</p> <h2>What you will do</h2> <ul> <li>Develop features requested by our customers</li> <li>Work with Red Hat’s Quality Engineering (QE) team to ensure that code changes to LLVM and LLDB are tested correctly</li> <li>Work on second-level bug reports, identify bugs, and get them fixed</li> <li>Submit and review patches for bug fixes to and from the community</li> <li>Educate the team on new features via small team presentations</li> </ul> <h2>What you will bring</h2> <ul> <li>Software development experience</li> <li>Knowledge of and experience with C, C++, and Python</li> <li>Passion for (or interest in) open source development</li> <li>Good communication skills, sense of accountability, and willingness to learn</li> </ul> <p>The following are considered a plus:</p> <p> </p> <ul> <li>Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field, or equivalent working experience</li> <li>Further experience in the software industry</li> <li>Experience with LLDB or LLVM</li> <li>Experience with the upstream LLDB or LLVM communities</li> </ul> <h2>About Red Hat</h2> <p>Red Hat is the world’s leading provider of enterprise open source software solutions, using a community-powered approach to deliver reliable and high-performing Linux, hybrid cloud, container, and Kubernetes technologies. Red Hat helps customers integrate new and existing IT applications, develop cloud-native applications, standardize on our industry-leading operating system, and automate, secure, and manage complex environments. Award-winning support, training, and consulting services make Red Hat a trusted adviser to the Fortune 500. As a strategic partner to cloud providers, system integrators, application vendors, customers, and open source communities, Red Hat can help organizations prepare for the digital future.</p> <p> </p> <p> </p><br><p><i>By submitting your interest in this job, you agree to receive text notifications with additional steps to complete your job application.
