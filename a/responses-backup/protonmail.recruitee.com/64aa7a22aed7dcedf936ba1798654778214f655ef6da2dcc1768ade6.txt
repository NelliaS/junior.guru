          <h2 class="title">
            Machine Learning Engineer
          </h2>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job description
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <p>A better internet is possible, one that empowers people to choose how their personal information is used. This might seem daunting, or even impossible, but at Proton, building this better internet is what we do every day.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>ProtonMail was founded in 2014 by a group of scientists who met at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN). Our first product, <strong>ProtonMail</strong>, is now the world’s largest encrypted email service. Subsequent Proton products, such as <strong>ProtonVPN</strong>, <strong>Proton Calendar</strong>, and <strong>Proton Drive</strong>, use end-to-end encryption that gives our users full control over how and with whom their data is shared.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Today, Proton is making privacy universally accessible to more than 50 million registered users, including journalists, some of the world’s largest organizations, and people in more than 180 countries. Our diverse and dynamic team is made up of more than 400 members representing  over 30 different nationalities. While we are based in Geneva, Switzerland, we have offices in Zurich, Prague, London, Vilnius, Skopje, Taipei, and many more employees working remotely around the world. </p>
<p><br></p>
<p>New York Times journalists, some of the world’s largest corporations, and millions of ordinary citizens use our products so they can choose who, if anyone, sees their information. Join us at one of Europe’s fastest-growing companies to help us solve challenging problems and build new products that will reach hundreds of millions of people. We want to create more than just one of the world’s most impactful tech companies, we want to create a new internet that serves the interests of all people. We need you, your voice, your ideas, and your ambition to make it happen.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">About the role</span></strong><br></p>
<p>At Proton, we use machine learning in our efforts to combat abuse and spam. In order to meet the demands of scale, we approach machine learning challenges from a system engineering standpoint, pushing the boundaries of scalable computing, tying together numerous data sources to build models that protect our users and reputation. We are seeking an ML Engineer to join our team and help advance our detection capabilities.<span style="font-size:18px;"><br></span><br></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">What you will do</span></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and deploy highly scalable classifiers and tools leveraging machine learning, regression, and rule-based models;</li>
<li>Creation of tools to help in model building, debugging, and explanation;</li>
<li>Ensuring the above models meet expected performance levels;</li>
<li>Adapt standard machine learning methods to best exploit modern parallel environments (e.g. distributed clusters);</li>
<li>Suggest, collect and synthesize requirements and create effective roadmaps;</li>
<li>Consistently advance the state of ML for your problem area; and</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with stakeholders, engage with other engineers, analysts, and customer support.<strong><br></strong>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
</ul>
          </div>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job requirements
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <ul>
<li>MS degree in Computer Science or related quantitative field with experience in machine learning related work or research, or PhD degree in Computer Science or related quantitative field;</li>
<li>Research and/or work experience in machine learning, pattern recognition, signal processing, data mining, artificial intelligence, or information retrieval;</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of statistics;</li>
<li>Expert knowledge developing production level ML products;</li>
<li>Strong software engineering skills;</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of Python;</li>
<li>Experience of demonstrating technical leadership working with teams, owning projects, and defining and setting technical direction for projects</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">Bonus points for</span></strong></p>
<ul><li>Experience in anti-abuse or spam detection</li></ul>
<p><strong><strong><br></strong></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">Why you should join Proton</span></strong></p>
<ul><li>
<strong><span style="font-size:18px;"></span></strong><strong>Be part of a movement - </strong>Proton is not just a product or service but a community-driven movement united by a shared vision of online freedom. Our services are open source, audited, and supported by community contributions. We give back to our community by maintaining core encryption libraries and by supporting other organizations furthering the same goals as us.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Work with smart and dedicated people - </strong>Our team is diverse, collaborative, and tight-knit with people coming from all walks of life, including many of the world’s top academic institutions and organizations, such as MIT, Harvard, Stanford, Caltech, Cambridge, and ETH.</li>
<li>
<strong>Join a strong brand - </strong>Our encrypted email service - ProtonMail - has grown to be a staple of online security and privacy. Proton has been featured in multiple popular television and film productions, such as Mr. Robot, Knives Out, Sounds of Metal, and more.</li>
<li>
<strong>Grow with us - </strong>We’re one of Europe’s fastest-growing startups, doubling in size every year. Our growth gives you limitless career and educational opportunities as well as the opportunity to work side-by-side with many world-leading experts in their fields.</li>
<li>
<strong>Have your voice heard - </strong>We value your opinion and encourage you to speak up and share your ideas and thoughts. At Proton, no problem is someone else’s problem. We collectively strive to do the right thing and be the undisputed best in the world at everything we do.</li>
</ul>
