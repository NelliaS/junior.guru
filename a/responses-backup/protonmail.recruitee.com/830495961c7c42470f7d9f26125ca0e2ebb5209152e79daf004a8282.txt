            Golang/C++ Engineer
          </h2>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job description
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <p>At Proton, we envision an internet where privacy is the default, and we’re building ways to help all people secure their digital lives and take control of their data. ProtonMail was founded in 2014 by a group of scientists who met at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN). And since its inception, the company has grown at a fast pace and today has over 40 million users.</p>
<p>Our principal product, <strong>ProtonMail</strong>, is the world’s largest secure email service. Our second product, <strong>ProtonVPN</strong>, is one of the world’s most popular VPN services. Our vision is to build an internet platform for the protection of digital rights, and we are also developing additional products, including <strong>ProtonCalendar,</strong> <strong>ProtonDrive</strong>, and more to come.</p>
<p>Our users include New York Times journalists, some of the world’s largest corporations, and millions of ordinary citizens. The world is adopting our products at an accelerating pace. Whether it’s in New York, or at the front lines of the global struggle for freedom and democracy in Hong Kong, Belarus, and Myanmar, the work we do saves lives and is transforming the world. Our team is diverse and dynamic, representing over 30 different nationalities, with offices in Geneva, Zurich, Prague, London, Vilnius, Skopje, and Taipei, employing people across the world.</p>
<p>Proton is a mission driven company. We’re deeply committed to enabling freedom online and ensuring the internet serves the interests of all people. Our technologies provide trusted ways for people to be secure online and in control of their information at all times. The world is adopting our products at an accelerating pace. Help us bring it from tens of millions of users, to billions of users. We’re creating one of the world’s most impactful tech companies.<br></p>
<p><strong><strong><br></strong></strong></p>
<p><strong><strong><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">Purpose of the role</span></strong></strong></strong><br></p>
<p>We are looking for a desktop application developer for Proton Bridge. Proton Bridge integrates your ProtonMail account with popular email clients, such as Microsoft Outlook, Mozilla Thunderbird, or Apple Mail. The application runs in the background by seamlessly encrypting and decrypting messages as they enter and leave your computer. Bridge is compatible with most email clients supporting IMAP and SMTP protocols. You can read more about the project here: <a href="https://github.com/ProtonMail/proton-bridge" rel="noopener">https://github.com/ProtonMail/proton-bridge</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">What you will do</span></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Automate native email client interaction with Proton Bridge</li>
<li>Develop and maintain the interface between Proton Bridge and Proton API</li>
<li>Take an active part in designing and developing new features</li>
<li>Analyze the MIME structure of messages</li>
<li>Optimize the performance of local servers</li>
</ul>
<ul>
</ul>
          </div>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job requirements
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <ul>
<li>Experience with object-oriented compiled languages, such as Golang, C++, C#, or Rust</li>
<li>Experience developing and testing desktop applications</li>
<li>Comfortable using collaboration and developer efficiency tools such as git, gitlab CI, etc.</li>
<li>An understanding of the principles of UI design and user experience</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">Technologies we use and teach</span></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Languages: Golang, C++, C#, make, bash</li>
<li>Frameworks: Qt with QML</li>
<li>Platforms: Windows, macOS, Linux</li>
<li>Collaboration tools: git, gitlab, JIRA, Confluence</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span style="font-size:18px;">Why you should join Proton </span></strong><br></p>
<ul>
<li><p>We work on interesting, challenging problems every day. There is never a dull moment.</p></li>
<li><p>Our startup culture means everyone’s opinion matters. You can have a significant impact.</p></li>
<li><p>Our rapid growth means there are many opportunities for advancement.</p></li>
<li><p>The chance to do something meaningful. ProtonMail protects dissidents, journalists, and activists. Our work saves lives.</p></li>
<li><p>Be part of something bigger. Millions of people and businesses around the world have embraced our idea of an Internet where privacy and security are the default. Join the privacy revolution that is changing the world.</p></li>
</ul>
