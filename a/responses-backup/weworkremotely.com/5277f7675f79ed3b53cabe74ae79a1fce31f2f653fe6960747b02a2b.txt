You will report directly to Buffer’s CTO.</div><div> </div><div>Our infrastructure team has highly tenured engineers that work across DevOps, site reliability, security, infrastructure management, and overall developer workflow. We aspire to build a platform for self-serve DevOps for our product engineers.
