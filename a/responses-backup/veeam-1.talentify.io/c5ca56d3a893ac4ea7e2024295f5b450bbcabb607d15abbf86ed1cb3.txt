<div><h1>Company Description</h1> </div><div><p>Everything’s in place for you to win at Veeam – the global leader in Cloud Data Management. We provide trusted back-up solutions that deliver cloud data management and protection, keeping the world moving for over 360,000 customers including the vast majority of Fortune 500 companies. We’re extremely successful: a billion-dollar company and Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant that’s won over 170 top industry awards. But we’re always looking forward. Everyone here plays a part in finding new opportunities and winning new deals, and you’ll be backed by a best-in-service product and an unrivalled reputation for delivering customer satisfaction – our net promoter score is 3.5x the industry average.</p> <p>Ultimately though, we grow together, so we’ll support you fully to be successful in your role. We’ll invest in you through our on-demand learning systems. Mentoring, training and coaching will help you to find your feet, take big challenges in your stride and perform at your best. There are acceleration programmes that could propel you further forward than you imagined. And whether it’s learning additional skills, gaining a new experience or taking the next step in your career, there will be lots of scope for development.</p> <p>All this in a place where people talk from the heart. We have a culture of focus and excellence. We encourage innovation and iteration. And since our achievements are tangible, we can keep it real and be genuine with each other. We’re inclusive, diverse, open and honest people who collaborate, support each other and have fun together. And we’re nimble enough for people to speak up. We play to win; we’re competitive, hungry and driven, but we remain humble. If that’s you, get ready to do Veeamazing thing<br /><br /><strong>About our team:</strong> </p><p>We are the leader in Kubernetes Backup and Disaster Recovery. K10, our data management platform purpose-built for Kubernetes, provides enterprise operations teams an easy-to-use, scalable, and secure system for backup/restore, disaster recovery, and application mobility with unparalleled operational simplicity. We're hiring for this role across all locations in the Czech Republic.<br />Working from our office or remotely - it's up to you to decide.</p> </div><br /><div><h1>Job Description</h1> </div><div><ul><li>You will own (design, develop in Go, and test) significant features in the K10 product related to backup and recovery of Kubernetes applications, databases, and storage systems;</li> <li>Build deep expertise in everything "cloud-native" - you will be working with state-of-the-art technologies as well as building a product that makes enterprise customers succeed in their "cloud-native" transformation;</li> <li>You will work with and contribute to the cloud-native open source community.</li> </ul></div> <br /><div><h1>Qualifications</h1> </div><div><ul><li>Expertise in developing, testing, and debugging production-quality, scalable, multi-threaded, concurrent systems;</li> <li>Experience developing enterprise solutions in a high-level programing language (C++, Java, C#, ect.)</li> <li>Experience with distributed systems concepts and client-server architectures;</li> <li>Strong computer science fundamentals (data structures, algorithms, and concurrency);</li> <li>You are passionate about code quality, extensibility, coding standards, testing, and automation;</li> <li>You are impact and result-driven and biased towards execution;</li> <li>BS, MS or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or equivalent industry experience.</li> </ul><p><strong>Nice To Have:</strong> </p><ul><li>Experience developing in Go;</li> <li>Experience with databases and/or storage systems - especially in the public cloud;</li> <li>Experience developing for and on containers, container scheduler, and orchestration platforms;</li> <li>Experience participating and contributing to open source.</li> </ul></div> <br /><div><h1>Additional Information</h1> </div><div><p><strong>We offer:</strong> </p><ul><li>Excellent professional and career opportunities in the international team of talented and energetic people;</li> <li>Competitive salary and regular pay rises;</li> <li>Generous benefits package from the very first day of your employment including: premium health insurance program for you and your children, additional payments for sick leave, English classes, meal tickets, MultiSport card, public transport pass, cell phone contract, and many others;</li> <li>Flexible working hours, no bureaucracy or formalism;</li> <li>Professional trainings and education, including courses and conferences, internal meetups and unlimited access to online library;</li> <li>Assistance arranging a work permit;</li> <li>Relocation package for non-Prague candidates: a one-time additional salary payment.</li> </ul><p><strong>Please note:</strong> <br />If the applicant is permanently present outside of the Czech Republic, Veeam reserves the right to refuse to consider the application for a job. Remote job during pandemic is only possible in case the employee is located in the Czech Republic.</p> </div><br /><br><p><i>By submitting your interest in this job, you agree to receive text notifications with additional steps to complete your job application.
