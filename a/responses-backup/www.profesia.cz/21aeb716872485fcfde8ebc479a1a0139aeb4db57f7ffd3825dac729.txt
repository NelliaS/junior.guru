Because of our unique position as the “glue” between the network and IT, our Software Engineers and Architects are experts in both networking AND software development technology, generating over 50 U.S. patents per year on topics like Intent-based Networking, Self-assembly of Virtual Network Functions, and Artificial Intelligence applied to network management. This position is required for AT&T’s Network transformation strategic direction.<br />
<br />
This position is required for AT&T’s Network transformation strategic direction since today and going forward NEO has greater need for innovation with Network Strategy & Support, 5G, Fiber, Win the Home to provide high-value technology and solutions. The role will influence the delivery of system automation capabilities across AT&T enterprise architects, engineers and managers across business units within the company. It will drive delivery of system automation enablers for key CTO and business initiatives 5G, RAN, 5G Core/NC, FN, SDN, Fiber, ROADM, Win Home. This includes driving towards a single source of truth network inventory platform enabled by leading industry CMDB platforms. This role will be integral in the Network Transformation initiatives driving new strategies, implementation of tooling and leading change.<br />
<br />
<br />
Position Summary:<br />
    Works closely with the Solution Architects and System Engineers to ensure that overall program and product strategies align with business and technical objectives<br />
    The key focus is to enable the automation of organization in all facets including but not limited to the following areas: development, quality assurance, and solution engineering.<br />
    Responsible for the creation of new technology infrastructures and maintains configuration management and automation tools.<br />
    Facilitates important test design decisions for SAFe Trains, business unit projects & program needs.<br />
    This role will be data driven and require metrics to be produced to show an increased efficiency in the organization.<br />
    This position is within Network Systems and technologies that enable the transformation of AT&T leading edge networks and services, such as 5G and fiber broadband.<br />
    Must be coupled with strong industry standard tooling knowledge and the strategic direction of automation.<br />
<br />
Principal Responsibilities<br />
    Participate in planning, definition, and high-level design of the system solution and explore alternatives<br />
    Develop the Architectural Runway in support of new business Features and Capabilities<br />
    Work with portfolio stakeholders, Solution and Enterprise Architects, to develop, analyze, split, and realize the implementation<br />
    Participate in project planning, system and solution demos, retrospective workshops<br />
    Understand technical characteristics of and assist with architectural integration of external components, systems, and platforms<br />
<br />
Skills:<br />
    Programming Languages - JAVA, Python<br />
