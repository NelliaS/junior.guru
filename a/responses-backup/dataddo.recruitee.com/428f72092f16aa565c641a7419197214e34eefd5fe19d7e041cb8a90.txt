          <h2 class="title">
            Go Developer - junior/medior/senior
          </h2>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job description
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <h3>
<strong><br></strong><strong>What you’ll do:</strong>
</h3>
<ul><li>As a Go developer in the <strong>Backend team</strong>, you will mainly collaborate on the development of a platform for data extraction and writing. Because we <strong>connect to a variety of third-party systems</strong>, the entertainment and <strong>new technologies</strong> you encounter during your work are taken care of.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You can <strong>use both classic and obscure technologies</strong>, and thanks to that <strong>you can constantly evolve as a developer</strong>.</li></ul>
<ul><li>If you wish, your role in the team may also include working with K8S clusters and managing them. So if you have experience in this area, this is definitely a <strong>plus for you</strong>!</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Because we are still a <strong>relatively small team</strong>, we have to <strong>make our work as easy as possible</strong>. That is why we try t<strong>o automate the development</strong> process and deployment, and we would like to see the same effort on your part.</li>
<li>But the main thing for us is the <strong>willingness to learn new things and the ability to overcome obstacles</strong>. <strong>We build on a green field and learn new things every day.</strong>
</li>
<li>If you want, we'll show you the code and it will click. <strong>Want to know more?</strong> Try to find the answers in the attached FAQ.</li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
          </div>
          <h3 class="alted">
            Job requirements
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
            <h2>FAQ</h2>
<p>--------</p>
<p><strong>Q: Do you expect me to know everything you work with?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> No. We expect you to be able to program, at least have theoretical knowledge of networks, SW design and practical experience with implementation in the go language. With this foundation and the desire to develop further, we welcome you to work with any of the components.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Are you testing?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> Yes, unit coverage and integration tests are a matter of course. On the other hand, we do not retest code that has already been tested by someone else.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What is the flow of development?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> We use CI / CD in Gitlab, Kubernetes' own test and production environment is deployed using Helm and Argo. We do a code review before each merger, thanks to which we, as individuals and as a team, are constantly moving on.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What equipment do you use?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> According to the developer's preferences. You choose the Macbook / Dell XPS, monitor and other peripherals at your own discretion.<br></p>
<p><strong>Q: What are the learning opportunities?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> In addition to being happy to teach you the technologies we use, we also offer the opportunity to travel to conferences and trainings. Personal growth is very important to us.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How big is the team?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A: </strong>A small team of quality developers and the opportunity to influence the product are waiting for you. In the Go team, we are so far 4. In the SW team, there are over 20 in total and there are over 40 of us in the company.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Where will I work from?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> We can offer work from our offices in Prague at Můstek, a hybrid model (alternating office / home office), or fully remote. It is up to you. </p>
<p><strong>Q: What benefits do you offer?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> In the office you get coffee, drinks, IPU and snacks so that he is not hungry and works well. We also have a sports club and it's up to you to join. 5 weeks of holidays but if it's not enough for you, we always make a deal. We are not machines, we are humans.<br><br></p>
<h3><strong>Who we are?</strong></h3>
<p>We are the Czech startup <strong>Dataddo</strong> and our main goal is to create our own global product for data transfer across various systems and platforms. We use modern technologies and rely on precisely done work and open communication.</p>
<p><strong>We develop a robust backend through</strong> which customer data flows and we ensure that even a non-technical customer is able to interconnect their systems and transfer data between them within a few minutes.</p>
<p>Dataddo is a recognized as a<strong> leader in data integration solutions</strong>. Companies like <strong>Uber Eats, Epic Games, Ogilvy, and L’Oreal</strong> trust Dataddo to help them instantly connect their data sources to dashboards and storages. <strong>We’re listed in the top 25 Czech startups</strong> to watch and <strong>selected as a Gartner Cool Vendor</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
