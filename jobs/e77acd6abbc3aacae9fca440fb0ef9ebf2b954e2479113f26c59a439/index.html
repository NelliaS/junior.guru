Area Manager, Network Engineering

AT&T — Brno

AT&T has been changing the way people live, work, and play for more than 140 years. It started with Alexander Graham Bell’s telephone. Since then, our legacy of innovation has included the invention of the transistor – the building block of today’s digital world – as well as the solar cell, the communications satellite and machine learning. Today, we are leading the industry in cutting edge networking technologies such as Software Defined Networking, Disaggregated Network Operating Systems and High-Speed Optical Network Connections. We were just recently awarded “America’s Best 5G Network” by Global Wireless Solutions and have been ranked #1 in Fiber Broadband Customer Satisfaction by J.D. Power. AT&T Labs, a global leader in telecommunications research and development, has been at the heart of these incredible achievements – drawing on an unparalleled 140-year heritage of creation and innovation.

This Business Unit is responsible for development and maintenance of AT&Ts Network Operational Support Systems (OSS) – the software applications that manage AT&Ts network. Because of our unique position as the “glue” between the network and IT, our Software Engineers and Architects are experts in both networking AND software development technology, generating over 50 U.S. patents per year on topics like Intent-based Networking, Self-assembly of Virtual Network Functions, and Artificial Intelligence applied to network management.

We are excited to announce the expansion of our team to Brno, Czech Republic. We’re looking for a manager to run a team of both experienced and new Software Engineers eager to join us in building and maintaining the next generation of Fiber / Wireless telecommunications network management software.

Job Role

This is a people management job with responsibility for supervising the work of highly technical teams involved in design and development solutions in a Research and Development environment for high-value technology tied to the strategic direction of AT&T.

Successful candidate manages highly technical teams who will be able to conduct design and development work in the areas of new products/technologies, systems/platform architecture, hardware/software development engineering, technical security, and QA Design/Certification. Works with new and emerging technologies and technical architectures on projects across the full life cycle.

Required Skills

  • People Management skills and experience
  • Excellent English as well as written and verbal communication skills
  • Business Orientation
  • Capacity Planning and Management
  • External Resource Management
  • Network Operations/Performance Management
  • Project Management
  • Exposure to Telecommunication Regulatory Environments
